Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bootcamp programs.

Can I access course materials offline?

Yes! All our course materials are available for download so you can learn even without an internet connection. You'll have lifetime access to the materials after completing the bootcamp.

Is there any prerequisite for courses?

Most of our beginner-friendly Courses require no prior experience. For intermediate/advanced programs, we recommend basic familiarity with the subject or completion of our prep courses.

How long do I have access to a course?

You'll have lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates. Our career support services are available for 12 months after graduation.

How can I contact the course instructor?

Each Course has a dedicated Slack channel where you can ask instructors questions. You'll also have weekly 1:1 office hours and can schedule additional time as needed.
